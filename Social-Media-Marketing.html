Social Media Marketing

Building brand presence and engagement across social platforms

What is Social Media Marketing?

Social media marketing is the use of platforms like Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, and X (Twitter) to promote products, engage with audiences, and drive traffic to your site or services.

Core Elements:

  • Organic Content: Posts, stories, reels, tweets
  • Paid Ads: Sponsored campaigns for visibility
  • Community Engagement: Comments, DMs, live sessions
