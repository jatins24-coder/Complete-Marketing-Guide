Traditional Marketing

Exploring the foundational forms of marketing

What is Traditional Marketing?

Traditional marketing refers to promotion strategies that have been used since the inception of advertising. These include print ads, broadcast (TV and radio), direct mail, telephone, and outdoor advertising like billboards and flyers.

Key Components:

  • TV and Radio: Wide reach but expensive
  • Print Media: Newspapers, magazines, brochures
  • Outdoor Advertising: Billboards, transit ads
  • Direct Mail: Postcards, catalogs
