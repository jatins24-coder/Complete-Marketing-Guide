Network Marketing

What is Network Marketing?

Network Marketing, also known as Multi-Level Marketing (MLM), is a strategy that relies on person-to-person sales by independent representatives, often working from home.

Key Strategies:

  • Referral Incentives: Motivate reps to expand networks
  • Product Demos: Personal selling to build trust
  • Training Systems: Equip reps with tools for growth
