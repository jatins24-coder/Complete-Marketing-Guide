Guerrilla Marketing

Unconventional tactics that spark attention and buzz

What is Guerrilla Marketing?

Guerrilla Marketing is a creative, cost-effective strategy that uses unconventional methods to promote a product or service. It relies on surprise and imagination to grab attention in unexpected places.

Key Strategies:

  • Street Art: Murals, graffiti, and visual statements in public spaces
  • Flash Mobs: Group performances to create viral moments
  • Ambient Marketing: Altering the environment to fit brand messaging
