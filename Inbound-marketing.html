What is Inbound Marketing?

Inbound marketing is a business methodology that attracts customers by creating valuable content and experiences tailored to them. It forms lasting connections by solving problems and building trust.

Key Strategies:

  • Content Creation: Blogs, videos, and guides that provide value
  • SEO: Making content discoverable organically
  • Email Nurturing: Converting leads through targeted messaging
