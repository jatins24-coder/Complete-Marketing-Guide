Affiliate Marketing

Earning commissions through referrals and promotions

What is Affiliate Marketing?

Affiliate marketing is a performance-based marketing strategy where individuals earn a commission by promoting other people's or companies' products. Affiliates share unique links and earn money for every sale or action generated via their referral.

Key Elements:

  • Affiliate: Promoter of the product
  • Merchant: Seller or creator of the product
  • Customer: End user who makes the purchase
  • Affiliate Network: Platform managing the relationship (optional)
